Research

My recent research has focused on exposure to social and environmental stressors, and how racial/ethnic differences in these exposures related to health outcomes across the lifespan.

Specially, I am interested in persistent racial/ethnic health disparities in the United States. In the broader context of the United States, exposure to stressors differs by racial/ethnic group, and the experience of and consequences from stress also differs by racial/ethnic group.

By recognizing these racial/ethnic differences in stress, I have begun to identify culturally relevant factors that may reduce health disparities in the United States.
